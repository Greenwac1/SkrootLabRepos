Sensors

The SKROOT platform comes in three parts:


Three easy steps for use: (1) place sensor sticker on reactor, (2) place reader near sensor, (3) power it on.


Data can be observed from any internet enabled device. Critical time-points (feed, harvest, induction) are pushed via text-message or email to operators. Numerous readers can be connected to a single cloud gateway enabling large number of vessels to be monitored; this is ideal for upstream process development.


Sensor Availability - testing with BETA Partners

  • Cell density
  • Cell viability
  • Conductivity (media preparation)
  • Temperature

Email us at info@skrootlab.com if you are interested in testing our technology at your facility.

Technology Features

  • Conformable, flexible
  • Placed as an external sticker
  • Can be embedded in single use bioreactors
  • Passive (no on board power)
  • Sterilizable (gamma radiation or autoclave)
  • Wireless, cloud enabled
  • Can be hardwired for established control systems (4-24 mA control)
  • Multiplex ease
  • Suited for upstream, small reactor use
